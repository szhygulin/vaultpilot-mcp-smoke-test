EigenLayer is a Ethereum-based restaking protocol that lets users stake their ETH (or liquid-staking tokens like stETH) into external validator networks beyond Ethereum itself, earning additional yield while contributing to network security. Participants deposit LSTs into EigenLayer and receive restaking rewards on top of base staking APR. The protocol launched in beta 2024 and is in active expansion for new operator onboarding.

EigenLayer carries liquidation risk — if an operator you delegate to is slashed, your stake is at risk. Current operators include Lido, Polygon, and Eigen Labs' own set.
