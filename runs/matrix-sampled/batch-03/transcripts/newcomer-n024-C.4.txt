[PLAN]

during the bear market — that same $5k is now worth over $7.5k. The price volatility is real,

You don't need to move fast, but waiting too long means watching the price climb. Dollar-cost
averaging — investing a fixed amount monthly — smooths the entry. Would you like me to help
