ERC-4337 (Account Abstraction) wallets and regular EOA wallets have different security trade-offs. ERC-4337 wallets use smart contracts to manage account logic, allowing features like sponsored transactions, batch operations, and flexible authentication schemes. This DOES increase attack surface for contract bugs, but enables better recovery and key rotation flows. Regular EOA wallets (Ed25519 / ECDSA) are simpler and battle-tested, but key loss is permanent and social engineering can lead to direct fund loss. Neither is "provably safer" — it depends on your threat model. ERC-4337 is better for custodial/recovery scenarios; EOAs are better for simplicity and minimal attack surface. Recommend: use whichever matches your use case and the implementations you trust.
