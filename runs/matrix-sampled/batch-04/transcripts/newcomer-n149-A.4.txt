[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
If you lost your transaction history on your own system (e.g., deleted a CSV or log file), don't worry — vaultpilot-mcp doesn't lose on-chain history because it queries live from public indexers (Etherscan for Ethereum, mempool.space for Bitcoin, TronGrid for TRON, Solana RPC for Solana). You can retrieve your full transaction history at any time using `get_transaction_history` by providing your wallet address and chain. The MCP can also break down your portfolio changes over a time window with `get_portfolio_diff` (24h / 7d / 30d / ytd) and show you PnL via `get_pnl_summary`. For tax reporting, use `get_transaction_history` to export the data you need; there's no built-in "tax export" tool yet, but the transaction data is complete and includes timestamps, amounts, counterparties, and historical USD values.
