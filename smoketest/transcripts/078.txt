[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
Uniswap V2 spreads every liquidity provider's funds evenly across the entire price range from 0 to infinity using a simple x*y=k curve, so most of the capital just sits unused. Uniswap V3 lets LPs concentrate their liquidity in a chosen price band (e.g., ETH between $3k–$4k), which makes each dollar of liquidity work much harder and earn more fees — at the cost of needing to actively manage the range and risking the position going "out of range" (and stopping earning) when the price moves outside it. V3 also introduced multiple fee tiers (0.01% / 0.05% / 0.3% / 1%) so stable pairs and volatile pairs can pick an appropriate fee, whereas V2 hard-coded a single 0.3% fee for every pool.
