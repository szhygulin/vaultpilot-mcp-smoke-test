[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
Lido is one of the largest liquid staking protocols and has a strong track record, but it's not risk-free: main risks are smart-contract bugs, validator slashing (losses are socialized across all stETH holders, historically tiny - well under 0.1% of stake), and stETH depeg/liquidity risk during market stress. Slashing on Ethereum is rare and capped (typically ~1 ETH for an isolated offense, larger for correlated mass-slashing), and Lido spreads stake across many professional node operators to limit correlated exposure.
