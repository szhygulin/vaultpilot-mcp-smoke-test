[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
Most Solana failures come from congestion-era issues: too-low priority fees (your tx never gets included before the blockhash expires ~60-90s), too-tight slippage on swaps, or hitting the default 200k compute-unit cap on complex programs. Avoid them by attaching a priority fee sized to current network load, setting realistic slippage (0.5-1% on liquid pairs, more on thin ones), and — for swaps/DeFi — letting your wallet/router auto-tune compute-unit limits and retry on blockhash-expiry errors.
