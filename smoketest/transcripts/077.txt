[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
Liquid restaking lets you stake ETH (or an LST like stETH) into a protocol like EigenLayer to also secure other "AVS" services, and receive a liquid restaking token (LRT — e.g. eETH, ezETH, rsETH, pufETH) that stays usable as DeFi collateral. People are excited because it stacks ETH staking yield with extra AVS rewards and points/airdrops while keeping capital liquid — though it also stacks slashing, smart-contract, and depeg risks, and much of the current "yield" is still speculative points rather than realized fees.
