[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
BIP-137 only signs with P2PKH-style ECDSA keys (works for legacy 1... addresses, and hacky "fake-P2PKH" tricks for P2SH-P2WPKH/P2WPKH), while BIP-322 is a generic scheme that signs a synthetic "to_spend / to_sign" virtual transaction so it works natively for every address type including P2WPKH (bc1q), P2TR (bc1p), and multisig — your vaultpilot wallet's sign_message_btc currently uses BIP-137.
